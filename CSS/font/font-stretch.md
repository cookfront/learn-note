font-stretch
========

`font-stretch`属性从字体家族中选择一个normal、condensed或expanded的外观，它的作用是使字体拉伸或收缩。

 - 取值：normal | ultra-condensed | extra-condensed | condensed | semi-condensed | semi-expanded | expanded | extra-expanded | ultra-expanded
 - 初始值：normal
 - 应用于：所有元素
 - 继承：yes

绝对的关键字值其从窄至宽拥有下列顺序：

 - Ultra Condensed
 - Extra Condensed
 - Condensed
 - Semi Condensed
 - Normal
 - Semi Expanded
 - Expanded
 - Extra Expanded
 - Ultra Expanded
